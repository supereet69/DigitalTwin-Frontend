Digital Twin Platform for Metal Recovery

Bio-hydrometallurgical and chemical leaching process modeling for recovery of metals from spent lithium-ion battery black mass

This platform hosts physics-informed digital twins representing biological and abiotic leaching mechanisms. The twins integrate kinetic modeling, anomaly simulation, and operating condition optimization to support experimental analysis and process understanding.
