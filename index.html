DEPARTMENT OF CHEMICAL ENGINEERING

DIGITAL TWIN PLATFORM

Bio-Hydrometallurgical Metal Recovery from Spent Lithium-Ion Batteries

This platform hosts physics-informed digital twins representing biological and chemical leaching mechanisms for metal recovery from spent lithium-ion battery black mass. The system integrates kinetic modeling, anomaly simulation and optimization for process understanding and operational decision-making.
